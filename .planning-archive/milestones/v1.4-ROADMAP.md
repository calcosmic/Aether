# v1.4 Roadmap: Deep Cleanup & Simplification

**Goal:** Remove dead weight, consolidate duplicates, simplify structure — make the repo understandable at a glance.

## Problem Statement

The Aether repo has accumulated files across 25 phases of work. Many are:
- Dead duplicates (`.aether/agents/` = `.opencode/agents/`, etc.)
- Orphaned worktree-salvage files
- Completed planning phases
- Unclear what's "core" vs "archived"
