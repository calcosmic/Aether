## Tech Debt

### Phase 12: Success Capture & Colony-Prime Enrichment

- **build-full.md missing MEM-01 success capture blocks.** The monolithic playbook mirror (`build-full.md`) was not updated with the two `memory-capture "success"` blocks added to `build-verify.md` (chaos resilience, Step 5.7) and `build-complete.md` (pattern synthesis, Step 5.9). The split playbook path works correctly — only the monolithic mirror is out of sync. Impact: users running via `build-full.md` won't get success events in the learning pipeline.

### Phase 14: Decision-Pheromone & Learning-Instinct Verification

- **REQUIREMENTS.md checkboxes not updated.** All 7 requirement checkboxes still show `[ ]` Pending despite all being implemented and verified. Cosmetic only — does not affect functionality.
- **DEC-01 dedup is continue-time only.** Decision pheromones can briefly duplicate between `context-update` emission and `Step 2.1b` dedup during the same continue cycle. Intentional design choice documented in 14-01-SUMMARY.md — batching dedup at continue time avoids complicating the context-update hot path.

### Total: 3 items across 2 phases
